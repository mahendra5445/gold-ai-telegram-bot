# Privacy Policy — AI Scalper Pro

Last updated: 23 July 2026

AI Scalper Pro ("the Service") is an automated trading-signal bot that delivers market analysis for gold (XAUUSD) and forex pairs via Telegram and WhatsApp.

## Information We Collect

When you interact with the Service on WhatsApp, we store only:
- Your WhatsApp phone number, used solely to deliver the signals you subscribed to.
- Your subscription status (whether you have sent START or STOP).

We do not collect your name, email address, location, payment details, or the content of your messages beyond the commands START, STOP and STATUS.

## How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used for one purpose only: sending you the trading signals and status replies you requested. We do not use it for marketing or profiling.

## Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third party. Message delivery is handled by the WhatsApp Business Platform, operated by Meta Platforms, Inc.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your phone number is stored only while you are subscribed. Send STOP at any time and it is deleted immediately from our records.

## Security

Data is stored on our hosting provider's servers and is not publicly accessible. All communication with the WhatsApp API uses encrypted HTTPS connections.

## Children

The Service is not intended for anyone under 18 years of age.

## Financial Disclaimer

The Service provides automated technical analysis for educational and informational purposes only. It is not financial, investment, or trading advice. Trading foreign exchange and precious metals carries a high level of risk. You may lose some or all of your capital. Past performance is not indicative of future results. You are solely responsible for your own trading decisions.

## Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact the operator of this Service through the Telegram bot.
